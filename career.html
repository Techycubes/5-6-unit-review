His Career

Tengen eventually joined the Demon Slayer Corps, where he was trained to become a Hashira. His unique fighting style,
centered around sound and inspired by his shinobi background, set him apart. Using his heightened sense of hearing,
he developed a technique called Sound Breathing, which allows him to analyze and anticipate his opponents' movements.
As the Sound Hashira, Tengen was instrumental in numerous missions. His expertise was most prominently displayed during the Entertainment District Arc,
where he led the battle against the Upper Rank Six demons, Daki and Gyutaro. Despite being severely injured and poisoned,
Tengen showcased his immense strength, agility, and tactical ingenuity, ultimately contributing to the defeat of the powerful demon duo.
Following the intense battle, Tengen retired from active duty due to his injuries. Despite stepping down,
his influence remained strong within the Corps, and he continued to provide guidance and support when needed.

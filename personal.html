His personal life

Tengen’s personal life is as vibrant as his personality. He is married to three wives—Makio, Suma,
and Hinatsuru—whom he cherishes deeply. Unlike his upbringing, Tengen fosters a relationship with his wives
based on mutual respect, love, and trust. They are also skilled kunoichi who actively assist him during missions,
embodying the teamwork and solidarity of their bond.
Tengen’s flamboyant demeanor often masks his more thoughtful and compassionate nature.
He is deeply committed to protecting innocent lives and ensuring that his loved ones are safe and happy. His personal philosophy emphasizes living life to its fullest, a reflection of his resolve to rise above the darkness of his past and embrace a brighter, more meaningful future.
